Motivational Quran Verses with Lessons

Quran Verse Lesson
Indeed, with hardship comes ease. (94:6) Never lose hope; every difficulty has ease.
Do not despair of the mercy of Allah. (39:53) Even after mistakes, keep hope alive.
So be patient. Indeed, Allah is with the patient. (2:153) Patience is key; Allah supports the patient.
Indeed, Allah loves those who rely upon Him. (3:159) Do your best and trust Allah (Tawakkul).
And establish prayer; it prohibits immorality. (29:45) Regular prayers help control bad habits and focus your life.
